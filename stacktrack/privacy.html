Privacy Policy — StackTrack

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

StackTrack (“the App”) does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data—players, buy-ins, chip counts, and results—is stored locally on your device only.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal or usage data. The App contains no analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party data collection.

Storage

All information you enter in StackTrack is stored locally on your device. The App does not connect to external servers or cloud services to store your data.

Third-Party Services

StackTrack uses only native iOS frameworks. No third parties have access to your data.

External Links

Links to the App Store or our website are provided for convenience and do not transmit your in-app data.
